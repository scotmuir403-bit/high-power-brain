# Content Creation

> Building and maintaining a consistent content output across TikTok, LinkedIn, and YouTube.

## Current output targets

| Platform | Frequency | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| TikTok @scott.digital.madesimple | 14 videos/week (2/day) | Audience building, working-class AI |
| LinkedIn | 3 posts/week minimum | B2B lead gen for Digital Made Simple |
| YouTube — The Bloke's Guide to AI | Slow burn | Long-form, non-technical AI explainers |

## Content pillars (TikTok)

1. "AI on a night shift" — building a business with AI while working full-time
2. AI tools explained simply — how to use Claude with no tech knowledge
3. Building in public — real progress, real clients, no manufactured hype
4. Anti-guru — honest takes on what AI can and can't do for small businesses

## The content test

Before publishing anything: *"Would this land with a tired working dad scrolling at midnight after a long shift — or would he roll his eyes and scroll past?"* If he'd roll his eyes, rewrite it.

## Weekly anchor

**Sunday 8pm** — pull analytics, research viral trends, generate the 14-video weekly plan. If this runs, the week runs.

## What the content is NOT

- No income screenshots
- No fake urgency or countdown timers
- No "most people don't know this secret..."
- No manufactured inspiration
- No implying Scott is further ahead than he is
