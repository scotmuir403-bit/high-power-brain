### Offer Strategy & Pricing

  - [**Inspiration:** A competitor's $250k launch in 20 days with a similar bootcamp model validated the format.](https://fathom.video/share/ywKbKy5oeQp98Bzb-bmjVyoYeCqusRVj?tab=summary&timestamp=547.0)
  - [**Format:** A live, 6-day bootcamp (Mon/Wed/Fri for 2 weeks) to ensure content is always current, solving the rapid pace of AI development.](https://fathom.video/share/ywKbKy5oeQp98Bzb-bmjVyoYeCqusRVj?tab=summary&timestamp=825.0)
  - [**Pricing:** $1,500–$2,000. This mid-ticket price point justifies a sales team, which provides flexibility to test messaging and close sales.](https://fathom.video/share/ywKbKy5oeQp98Bzb-bmjVyoYeCqusRVj?tab=summary&timestamp=941.0)
  - [**Sales Process:** Webinar → Book a Call → Sales Team. This allows for a high-urgency close (e.g., "starts in 4 days") and offers payment plans.](https://fathom.video/share/ywKbKy5oeQp98Bzb-bmjVyoYeCqusRVj?tab=summary&timestamp=1115.0)
