### Strategy for a New High-Ticket Offer

  - [**Goal:** Launch a high-ticket offer to increase revenue without client-delivery work.](https://fathom.video/share/TL6gLZxJQzpAGkHVsbSJaZo18SLekqAe?tab=summary&timestamp=438.0)
  - [**Dane's Experience & Warning:**](https://fathom.video/share/TL6gLZxJQzpAGkHVsbSJaZo18SLekqAe?tab=summary&timestamp=508.0)
      - [**High-Ticket ($20k+):** High risk of legal issues from disgruntled clients, even with a strong service.](https://fathom.video/share/TL6gLZxJQzpAGkHVsbSJaZo18SLekqAe?tab=summary&timestamp=571.0)
          - [**Cost:** Dane's company spent $20k–$50k on attorney fees to defend against state investigations.](https://fathom.video/share/TL6gLZxJQzpAGkHVsbSJaZo18SLekqAe?tab=summary&timestamp=571.0)
      - [**Mid-Ticket ($3k–$10k):** The recommended "sweet spot." It offers higher value and revenue without the extreme overhead.](https://fathom.video/share/TL6gLZxJQzpAGkHVsbSJaZo18SLekqAe?tab=summary&timestamp=627.0)
  - [**Proposed Mid-Ticket Offer Structure:**](https://fathom.video/share/TL6gLZxJQzpAGkHVsbSJaZo18SLekqAe?tab=summary&timestamp=1743.0)
      - [**Core Value:** Provide direct access and execution support, which is what clients truly pay for.](https://fathom.video/share/TL6gLZxJQzpAGkHVsbSJaZo18SLekqAe?tab=summary&timestamp=1743.0)
      - [**Tiered Access Model:**](https://fathom.video/share/TL6gLZxJQzpAGkHVsbSJaZo18SLekqAe?tab=summary&timestamp=1190.0)
          - [**Low-Ticket ($89/mo):** Guides, skills, and monthly calls.](https://fathom.video/share/TL6gLZxJQzpAGkHVsbSJaZo18SLekqAe?tab=summary&timestamp=1190.0)
          - [**Mid-Ticket ($3k–$10k):** Weekly "build with me" calls, private chat, and direct access (e.g., cell phone number).](https://fathom.video/share/TL6gLZxJQzpAGkHVsbSJaZo18SLekqAe?tab=summary&timestamp=1743.0)
      - [**Dane's Upcoming Offer:** A quarterly cohort ($3.5k–$5k) with weekly live builds and support.](https://fathom.video/share/TL6gLZxJQzpAGkHVsbSJaZo18SLekqAe?tab=summary&timestamp=1108.0)
