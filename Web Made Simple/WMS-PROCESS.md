# Web Made Simple — Delivery Process

## Every Client Journey

Stage 1 — Lead (said yes to DM)
- [ ] Send intake questions
- [ ] Create client folder
- [ ] Fill brief.md

Stage 2 — Brief Complete
- [ ] All info in brief.md
- [ ] Write copy.md
- [ ] Scott approves copy

Stage 3 — Build
- [ ] Build index.html
- [ ] Run quality checklist
- [ ] Deploy preview to Vercel

Stage 4 — Review
- [ ] Send preview link to client
- [ ] Collect feedback
- [ ] Make changes, redeploy

Stage 5 — Launch
- [ ] Client approves
- [ ] Connect domain or use vercel URL
- [ ] Go live

Stage 6 — Case Study (free clients)
- [ ] Fill case-study.md
- [ ] Get written testimonial
- [ ] Screenshot before and after
- [ ] Plan TikTok content

## Intake Questions
1. Full business name
2. All services offered
3. Areas and postcodes covered
4. Phone number
5. WhatsApp number
6. Email for contact form
7. Address or area
8. Years trading
9. Photos of work available?
10. Logo available?
11. Colour preferences or examples
12. What do best customers say?
13. What makes them different?

## Pricing
FREE — full site, domain cost only (~£12/yr), in exchange for testimonial
PAID — £497 one-off, full custom site, own domain setup, 7 days
MAINTENANCE — optional £20/month, hosting, updates, small changes

## What Makes a Good Case Study
- Business name and trade
- The problem (no website, not found on Google)
- What was built
- The result (calls, enquiries, Google visibility)
- Direct client quote
- Before and after screenshots
