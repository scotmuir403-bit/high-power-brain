Most small business owners I speak to have the same problem with content.

They know they should be posting. They're just never sure what to write, and by the time they figure it out, it's 10pm and they're too tired to care.

That's not a creativity problem. That's a process problem.

An AI content system fixes it at the root. Here's what it actually involves:

**A brand brief.** One document that tells AI who you are, what you do, who you talk to, and what you never say. You write it once. It underpins everything.

**A weekly routine.** Every Sunday, AI takes the brief and generates a week of post ideas, outlines, and first drafts. Calibrated to your voice and your audience.

**A review step.** You spend about 20 minutes choosing what goes out and making edits. The posts sound like you — because the brief told AI who you are.

After the first few weeks, it runs. Week after week.

You're not outsourcing your voice. You're removing the blank page.

I built this system for myself eighteen months ago. I now build it for clients.

If you run a small business and content is always the thing that slips — this is the conversation worth having.
