Google announced over 100 AI things last week at I/O 2026.

Most of the coverage missed the three that actually matter to small business owners.

**1. AI Mode in Google Search is now rolling out globally — for free.**

Your customers are about to search differently. Longer questions. Conversational answers. Less clicking links. If your business isn't showing up in those AI-generated summaries, you're going to see a drop in traffic. This isn't future-talk. It's happening now.

**2. The Daily Brief Agent.**

It reads your goals, checks your calendar, and organises your day into priorities. Free. If you're a small business owner starting every morning unsure what to focus on — this is worth ten minutes to set up.

**3. NotebookLM.**

Upload your documents — service guides, FAQs, proposal templates — and ask it questions. It reads them and answers. For any business with a lot of information that needs turning into content, this is the one to start with.

None of these cost money to use today.

The gap between businesses using AI tools and those not is widening every month. Not because AI is doing the work for them — but because it's removing the friction that stops consistent action.

Which of these three would be most useful for how you work right now?
