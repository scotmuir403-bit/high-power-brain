I had my first proper client discovery call this week.

A small business owner. Good product. Consistent in their work. Zero consistent content.

Sound familiar?

The problem wasn't that they didn't know what to say. They had plenty to say. The problem was they had no system to say it reliably — and every time they sat down to write a post, it felt like starting from scratch.

That's the pattern I see again and again.

So here's what we talked about building together.

A simple brief that captures the business, the audience, and the voice. One document.

A weekly AI-assisted process that turns that brief into a week of content outlines and drafts — without them having to stare at a blank screen.

A light review step so the content that goes out still sounds like them.

The whole thing takes about three weeks to set up properly. After that, it runs.

No social media manager needed. No agency fees. Just a system that does the heavy thinking so they can focus on the work.

If you run a small business and you recognise that pattern — the knowing-you-should-post-but-never-quite-doing-it — that's what I help with.

Happy to have a conversation. No hard sell, no pitch deck.
