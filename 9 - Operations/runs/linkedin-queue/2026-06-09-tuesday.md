If you've ever sat staring at a blank page wondering what to post this week — here's what was actually wrong.

You were trying to think of ideas and write the post at the same time. Two different jobs, both happening in your head at once. No wonder it felt like wading through mud.

Here's the small change that fixed it for me:

Step one — ask AI for ten rough topic ideas. Nothing polished. Just a list to react to.

Step two — separately, pick three of those and ask it to turn just those into proper posts.

That's it. Two short prompts. About ten minutes total.

Splitting "what do I say" from "how do I say it" turned a task that used to eat my whole evening into something I can do on a tea break.

If you're a business owner who knows you should be posting more but the blank page keeps winning — try splitting the job in two before you try anything more complicated. It's the simplest fix I've found, and it's the one that actually stuck.

Happy to share the exact prompts I use if it'd help — just ask.
