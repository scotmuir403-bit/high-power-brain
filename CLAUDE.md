## Brand voice

**Sound like:** A bloke who's done the physical work, is still doing it, and is honest about where he actually is — not where he wishes he was.

**Core traits:** Straight-talking. Honest. Anti-guru. Relatable to working-class people. Warm but direct.

**In practice:** Short sentences. Plain words. Active voice. Never condescending — always peer-to-peer. Humour: dry, self-aware, working-class.

**What Scott never says:** "game-changing" / "life-changing" / "crushing it" / "my millionaire morning routine" / "most people don't know this secret" / anything with a countdown timer / anything that implies he's further ahead than he is.

**Content test:** Before publishing anything: *"Would this land with a tired working dad scrolling at midnight after a long shift — or would he roll his eyes and scroll past?"* If he'd roll his eyes, rewrite it.
