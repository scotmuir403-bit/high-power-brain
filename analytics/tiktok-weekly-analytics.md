**Angle that converted best:** "Building on the side" + physical worker identity + time savings — highest likes AND highest new followers. This is the sweet spot.

**Hook styles that got views:** Problem/fix ("lazy answers because of how you ask") and news hooks ("just made this significantly better") topped views.

**Myth-busting works:** "isn't what most people think" got strong likes (9) + solid views.

**Plain English + physical job framing** showing up in search queries — the positioning is landing.
